About Us

Founded in 1992 by master jeweler Alessandro Roda, Roda Gems began as a small family workshop in the heart of the jewelry district. With an unwavering commitment to excellence and a passion for precious stones, Alessandro started with a simple vision: to create jewelry that tells stories and captures moments that last forever.

Over three decades, we have grown from a modest atelier to an internationally recognized name in luxury jewelry. Our reputation is built on the foundation of exceptional craftsmanship, ethical sourcing, and personalized service that has been passed down through generations.

Today, Roda Gems continues to honor its heritage while embracing innovation, creating pieces that blend traditional techniques with contemporary design. Each jewelry piece we craft is a testament to our dedication to quality and our commitment to making every moment extraordinary.
